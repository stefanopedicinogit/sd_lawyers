About Us

Expert Legal Solutions for a Brighter Tomorrow

We are a law firm with an international presence, with offices in Lisbon (Avenida Almirante Reis) and Rio de Janeiro (Copacabana), specializing in strategic and personalized legal assistance for foreigners wishing to invest, reside, or start a business in Portugal and Brazil.

Founded by two lawyers with extensive legal experience and an international background, our work is distinguished by client proximity, ethics, confidentiality, and effective and secure legal solutions.

We support investors, entrepreneurs, families, and digital nomads in all phases of the immigration process, including company formation, obtaining visits, and regularization in both countries. We also operate in the areas of citizenship, real estate law, international contracts, and estate planning, with a transnational focus.

Our commitment is to simplify what seems bureaucratic, to welcome each client with personalized attention, and to build legal solutions that meet their projects and dreams.

Elenice Duarte

Transnational / International Lawyer

I am a licensed attorney, registered with the Bar Associations of Brazil and Portugal, with specialized expertise in assisting foreigners who wish to live, invest, or start a business in Portugal and Brazil.

My Main Areas of Practice
  • Foreigners' Law: with a focus on residence regularization, visa applications (including Golden Visa, D7, D2, and digital nomad visas), Portuguese citizenship procedures, and comprehensive consulting for investors and families in international mobility.
  • Civil and Contract Law: drafting purchase and sale contracts, international contracts, and transnational estate planning.
  • International Family Law: marriage, divorce, and custody proceedings involving Brazilian and foreign citizens, with a focus on homologations between Brazil and Portugal.
  • Commercial Law: incorporation of companies in Portugal and Brazil, including corporate analysis and legal support for foreign entrepreneurs.
My Background

I graduated in Law from the Higher Education Institute of Brasilia (2017) and began my career in Portugal in 2018, after permanently moving to the country. Since then, I have worked in various areas of law, developing a practice focused on transnational law, which combines legal expertise and cultural sensitivity.

Languages

I offer consultations in Portuguese (native language), English, and Spanish. I have direct experience with international clients, including a period of activity in the Netherlands, which has enriched my practice with a global legal perspective.

Additional Information

I am committed to providing personalized and efficient services to my clients, taking into account their specific needs and goals. If you need assistance with any of the areas mentioned above, please do not hesitate to contact me.
